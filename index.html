About Me

Passionate about AI for good

Oluwaseun Nifemi is an AI Engineer and Full-Stack Developer with over seven years of experience building and deploying impactful AI and software solutions. Specialising in MLOps, LLM-based systems, and multilingual AI applications, she has a proven track record of delivering scalable, production-ready platforms in education, governance, and healthcare across Africa.

At EqualyzAI, Oluwaseun led the creation of uLearn, an AI-powered learning platform designed to address the shortage of quality STEM education in underserved Nigerian schools. By integrating fine-tuned large language models and local-language content, uLearn expanded access to education for thousands of students, showcased at GITEX Africa 2024 and presented at the Global AI Summit Rwanda 2025.

Previously, at Data Science Nigeria (DSN), she fine-tuned NLP models for Yoruba-language science education, developed AI-powered chatbots, and implemented cloud-native APIs with LangChain and LlamaIndex. These projects strengthened her expertise in AI for low-resource languages and educational technology.

Oluwaseun combines her technical skills with leadership and mentorship, training over 150 learners in AI and full-stack development through programs like DeepTech Ready, IndabaX Nigeria, and Azure Volunteer initiatives. Her work has earned accolades including the Project Ownership Award and Most Resourceful Staff recognition.

A frequent presenter at international conferences such as ICLR, AfricaNLP, and the Deep Learning Indaba, Oluwaseun actively contributes to the global AI community, fostering cross-cultural collaboration and promoting ethical, responsible AI.

With deep expertise in Python, Node.js, FastAPI, AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, and machine learning frameworks, she blends AI engineering with full-stack development to create solutions that are innovative and socially meaningful.
